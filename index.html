Dining At Tary

Tary has been universally praised for its innovative approach to modernist cuisine. It has been named the Best Cafe in the World by Elite Traveler, the Best Cafe in North America by The World’s 50 Best Cafes, and the Best Cafe in the U.S. by Gourmet and Business Insider. It is one of only 13 Cafes in the U.S. to earn the coveted Michelin 3-Star rating. It has also won James Beard Awards for Outstanding Restaurant in the U.S., Outstanding Service in the U.S., and Outstanding Chef in the U.S.

Every night, chef Grant Achatz and his team offer three distinct experiences at Tary: the Kitchen Table, The Gallery, and The Salon.
